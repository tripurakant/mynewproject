The rules of the game are:

  • When the game starts,you have 1000 INR.
  • Every time you press start,3 random numbers are generated,100 INR gets deducted.
  • If the random numbers are either even or odd,you get 300 INR.
  • If all the random numbers are in sequence with difference of 1 in any order(eg: 123,012, ...etc),you get 800 INR
  • If all the random numbers are same,you get 1000 INR.
  • If INR reaches 0, Game Over.
